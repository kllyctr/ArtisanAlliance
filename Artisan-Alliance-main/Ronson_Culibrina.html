Ronson Culibrina | Modern Contemporary Artist

Know the Artist

Kilalanin


Ronson Culibrina (b. 1991) lives and works in the Philippines. Graduating with a degree in fine arts from the Technological University of the Philippines in 2011, Culibrina has been taking part and winning in art competitions nationwide since his student days. He has participated in a number of group shows and recently had his first solo exhibition titled Before the Sun Down at 1335 Mabini (Manila, The Philippines).
